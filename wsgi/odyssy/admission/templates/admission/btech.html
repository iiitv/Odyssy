Bachelor of Technology Admissions

Admissions to the four year B.Tech. Program in both the branches (IT & CSE) of the Institute is made through the examination conducted by CBSE under the name and style of JEE (Main). Official Notification of this examination is given wide publicity through national dailies during September-December every year. The examination is scheduled usually in May the following year and held at several centers spread over the entire length and breadth of the country. Based upon the merit in the written examination, Central Counseling Board of JEE (Main) invites candidates for counseling at selected centers and seats for various participating institutions are allotted, based upon individual merit.

Details about cutoffs are available at CSAB website. For any queries or details related to admissions, e-mail us at registrar@iiitvadodara.ac.in.

Fees Refund Policy

Bachelor's Degree Programs - Fees Refund Policy

The fees refund policy for candidates seeking admission in B.Tech Courses in IIIT Vadodara is as under:
1. In all cases where student has left the Institute based on the decision of JOSAA/ CSAB,

Rule A
(a) Tuition fee – INR 2,000/- to be charged as application processing fee. Remaining fees to be refunded.
(b) Hostel / Transport & Mess – 90% of Transport, Hostel and Mess fees to be refunded.
(c) 100% of caution money deposited be refunded.
(a) Other Cases (Student decides to cancel admission after joining the institute/ confirming JOSAA about admission:
(i) Case 1: If all seats in the respective category are filled even after withdrawal at the end of admission process, the Rule A as mentioned above to be applicable.
(ii) Case 2: If all seats in that category are not filled due to the withdrawal of the seat at the end of admission process or withdrawal after commencement of classes, then Rule B would be applicable.
Rule B
(i) Tuition feeSince, the seat accepted and later cancelled by the candidate: 100% tuition fees including sports and cultural fees and examination fees to be forfeited.
(ii) Hostel – NIL amount to be refunded.
(iii) Transport & Mess – Refund of remaining calendar month.
(iv) 100% Caution money deposited be refunded.
