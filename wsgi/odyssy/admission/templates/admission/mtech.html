Master of Technology Admissions

Admissions to the M.Tech. (CSE) Program is made through the Centralized Counselling for M.Tech./M.Arch./M.Plan./M.Des. admissions (CCMT). Official notification of the admissions is given wide publicity through national dailies during March-April every year. CCMT is on-line admission process for NITs and many IIITs including other national level institutions. It is a merit based admission considering valid GATE score and qualifying degree norms as declared by the CCMT. A candidate may participate in the CCMT admissions process through the official admission portal of the CCMT

Details about cutoffs, participating institutions, seat matrix, and more are available at CCMT 2018.
For any queries or details related to MTech admissions, e-mail us at pgadmission@iiitvadodara.ac.in.

Fees Refund Policy

Master's Degree Programs - Fees Refund Policy

The fees refund policy for candidates seeking admission in M.Tech Courses in IIIT Vadodara is as under:
1. In all cases where student has left the Institute based on the decision of CCMT,

Rule A
(a) Tuition fee – INR 2,000/- to be charged as application processing fee. Remaining fees to be refunded.
(b) Hostel / Transport & Mess – 90% of Transport, Hostel and Mess fees to be refunded.
(c) 100% of caution money deposited be refunded.
Other Cases (Student decides to cancel admission after joining the institute/ confirming CCMT about admission:
(i) Case 1: If all seats in the respective category are filled even after withdrawal at the end of admission process, the Rule A as mentioned above to be applicable.
(ii) Case 2: If all seats in that category are not filled due to the withdrawal of the seat at the end of admission process or withdrawal after commencement of classes, then Rule B would be applicable.
Rule B
(i) Tuition feeSince, the seat accepted and later cancelled by the candidate: 100% tuition fees including sports and cultural fees and examination fees to be forfeited.
(ii) Hostel – NIL amount to be refunded.
(iii) Transport & Mess – Refund of remaining calendar month.
(iv) 100% Caution money deposited be refunded.
